Virtual Reality

During the Fall 2022 semester of my graduate studies in Creative Technologies, I developed an XR application utilizing both Virtual Reality and Mixed Reality concepts. These applications were created using Unity Game Engine.

Musical Puzzles

The first project utilizing VR technologies was a puzzle game requiring players to carefully fit pieces into a large puzzle surface in front of the player. As the player completed parts of the puzzle, different sounds would begin to play. Once the puzzle piece was complete, a entire track would be playing that consisted of the individual audio sources coming together to form one cohesive musical score. The game design was implemented using Unity Game engine and was ran on the Meta Quest 2 VR headset. All of the audio programming, implementation and composition was done by me.

Adding Dimension

In the next iteration of the application, I added several additional features. Handtracking capabilites were incorporated utilizing Microsoft's "MRTK" VR toolkit and Unity. This iteration contained many of the same core mechanics as its previous versions but in this iteration, the player solves a 3D puzzle. The puzzle can be rotated and transformed using the player's hands in realtime. The progressive audio mechanics from the first version remained the same, as the player filled out the puzzle pieces different sounds would be triggered.
